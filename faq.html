Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Buy Now Pay Later (Klarna/Affirm). All payments are secure and encrypted.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping: 3-5 business days. Express shipping: 1-2 business days. Free standard shipping on orders over $49.

What is your return policy?

We offer 30-day hassle-free returns. Items must be in original condition with packaging. Return shipping is free for defective products.

Do you offer repairs?

Yes, we have authorized service centers nationwide. Mail-in repairs are also available. Contact support for a repair ticket.

Are products authentic?

100% authentic, directly sourced from manufacturers. We are an authorized reseller for all brands featured on our site.

How do I track my order?

Go to our Track Order page and enter your order ID. You'll receive real-time shipping updates.

How do I leave a review?

Visit our Reviews page to share your experience. We appreciate your feedback!
